ABOUT US

WE STARTED AS TWO FRIENDS WHO LOVED TO SNOWBOARD.

BUT OVERTIME WE NOTICED A PRETTY BIG PROBLEM WITH SNOWBOARDING

Everytime few times we went riding there was a noticeable negative change in quality of our base wax.

Of course everytime this happens we had to change and repair our wax.

BUT THIS TOOK OVER AN HOUR!!!

We decided that it was time to change things up and introduce a new way to wax a snowboard.

With the new Animal Snowboards base wax technology, waxing a snowboard is a simple streamlined experience

That saves time, and money.

So instead of worrying about your wax, you only need to worry about when to hit the slopes.
